The simplest way to track your Run169 progress

Focus on having fun after your races. We offer the easiest way to track your progress through Run169.

Create Your Account Sign In

We Submit Towns for You

When you create your account, you'll be asked to provide your Run169 details. Then, once you submit towns on our application we will submit the form on your behalf as you finish your races. One less thing for you to worry about and remember!

Add Friends

You can search and add friends to your Run169 account. This will allow you to share your progress with your friends and brag about your achievements.

Save Your Times

You can save your times for each race you complete. This will allow you to track your progress over time and see how you're improving. You can also share your times with your friends and compare your progress with theirs.

Find Races and Results

You can search for races near you or browse through our list of upcoming events. This will help you find races that fit your schedule and interests. If you ran a race that isn't on our list, you can add it yourself!

Share Your Profile

Since your profile will have a direct link, you'll be able to view your Connecticut Run Map as you make progress towards completing Run169. You can share this link with your friends and family to show them your progress.

Open Sourced

This project is open sourced on GitHub. You can view the code, contribute to the project, and even deploy your own instance of the app.
